WAP to find area of triangle whose sides are given

a=12
b=13
c=15
print("using heron's formula")
s=(a+b+c)/2
print("semi-perimeter of triangle is:",s)
area=(s*(s-a)*(s-b)*(s-c))**0.5
print("area of triangle is:",area)
